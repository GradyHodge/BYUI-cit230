Color Scheme

Using the psychology of color theory, our research is seeking colors that represent our message of authority regarding our services to protect them against harsh weather. This color scheme must give them a sense of forboding stormy weather at the same time, providing a sense of protection against stormy weather. A combination of 'modern' cold tones mixed with familiar warm skin tones can set the appropriate mood.

Color Theory

Cold Tones: gray, blue/green, white

Warm Tones: reds, oranges, yellows, browns
