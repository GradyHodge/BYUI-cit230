Audience Demographics

The Outdoor Enthusiasts

Target audience research has narrowed down a defined profile of ideal site users. This site is for outdoor enthusiasts who are serious about embracing anything outdoor conditions throw at them.

Our number one target type are runners, people who run multiple miles on a daily basis. Hikers, hunters, and hobbiests are also similar groups with many overlapping demographic qualities.

The most important aspect of this are those runners who participate in outdoor social events and make online purchases.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

An outdoor enthusiast picks up their phone late at night or early in the morning to check on the chances of it raining while they are out.

Site Services: They easily and quickly check the site for local weather conditions and recommendations on how to layer their clothing/gear

Scenario 2

A runner is planning for an event such as a road race at a national park and wants to prepare the appropriate gear for before, during, and after their race.

Site Services: They readily find a local forecast of the area's weather as well as what the ground conditions are like and how the local participants are preparing.

Scenario 3

A hiker is curious about what other local outdoor enthusiasts do to keep warm and dry and search the site for tips and fashionable trends.

Site Services: The user finds local social groups, clubs, communities, and events who all post blogs and recommendations on how to dress for harsh weather conditions and be most comfortable during an event.

Scenario 4

A local runners club has written a blog for a snowshoe running event.

Site Services: The blog link is posted with social events and their images of the event are posted in the gallery along with additional back links to their site.

Scenario 5

A site member has great photos of their beach run with pictures of a winter storm rolling in.

Site Services: images of individuals, local nature, and local events are posted and tagged in the gallery
