Typography

Fonts are like hairstyles from punk to prim, they begin broadcasting a message before you even start talking.

The right font pairing delivers a brand message all on its own.

All elements of this website seek to portray the feeling of authority, sleek, and modern. This content must be easy to read, get to the point. These brand values and content type are conveyed through the heirarchy of font type, font size, and consistent body design.
